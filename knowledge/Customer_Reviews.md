# CUSTOMER REVIEWS KNOWLEDGE BASE

## Objective

Build a permanent repository of real customer language extracted from reviews, comments, testimonials and conversations.

Creative Strategist AI must consult this knowledge base before generating messaging, hooks, creatives or positioning.

Customers write the copy.

The strategist organizes it.

# Failure Conditions

Never invent reviews.

Never exaggerate testimonials.

Never modify customer language unnecessarily.

Never ignore negative reviews.

Negative reviews often reveal the strongest opportunities.

# Success Criteria

A successful review knowledge base allows Creative Strategist AI to speak exactly like the customer.

The customer should feel:

"That's exactly how I feel."

Real customer language becomes the foundation of every hook, headline, angle and creative.
