Prioritize hooks that:

Create emotional identification.

Challenge existing beliefs.

Promise transformation.

Avoid obvious advertising language.

Increase scroll stopping.

# Failure Conditions

Reject hooks that:

Describe features.

Sound like advertisements.

Depend on clickbait.

Overpromise.

Require unrealistic claims.

Feel generic.

Could apply to any product.

---

# Success Criteria

A successful hook:

Stops scrolling.

Creates emotional identification.

Opens a curiosity gap.

Feels believable.

Leads naturally into the story.

Can be reused across multiple campaigns.

Becomes part of the permanent knowledge base.
