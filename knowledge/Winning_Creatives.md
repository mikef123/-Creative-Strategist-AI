# WINNING CREATIVES KNOWLEDGE BASE

## Objective

Build a permanent repository of high-performing advertising creatives.

Creative Strategist AI must analyze winning creatives before recommending new concepts.

The objective is not to copy creatives.

The objective is to identify repeatable success patterns.

Every winning creative becomes reusable strategic intelligence.

# Failure Conditions

Reject creatives that:

Cannot be replicated.

Require unrealistic claims.

Lack commercial intent.

Generate engagement without purchases.

Feel overly produced.

Ignore customer psychology.

# Success Criteria

A successful creative knowledge base allows Creative Strategist AI to recognize winning creative patterns before producing new recommendations.

Every new creative should inherit proven structures while introducing new hypotheses.

Creativity becomes systematic rather than random.
