# MARKET INSIGHTS KNOWLEDGE BASE

## Objective

Build a permanent repository of validated market observations, emerging trends and commercial opportunities.

Creative Strategist AI must consult this knowledge base before generating research, positioning, offers or creative recommendations.

Market insights are strategic intelligence.

They describe how markets evolve, not isolated campaign results.

Never document trends without evidence.
