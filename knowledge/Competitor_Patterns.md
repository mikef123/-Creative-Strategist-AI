# COMPETITOR PATTERNS KNOWLEDGE BASE

## Objective

Build a permanent repository of competitor strategies, messaging patterns, creative trends and positioning.

Creative Strategist AI must consult this knowledge base before recommending positioning, offers, hooks or creatives.

The objective is not to copy competitors.

The objective is to identify opportunities they leave unexploited.

Competitive intelligence is used to differentiate, not imitate.

# Failure Conditions

Never copy competitors.

Never assume competitors are correct.

Never imitate messaging without differentiation.

Never ignore weak competitors.

Weak competitors often reveal market opportunities.

---

# Success Criteria

A successful competitor knowledge base allows Creative Strategist AI to identify market gaps before creating strategy.

Every competitor becomes a source of insight rather than imitation.

Differentiation becomes systematic.
