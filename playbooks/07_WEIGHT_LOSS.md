# Testing Priorities

Test confidence before appearance.

Test identity before weight.

Test lifestyle before ingredients.

Test testimonials before scientific explanations.

Test long-term transformation before rapid results.

Test habit formation before fat loss.

---

# Compliance Notes

Avoid guaranteed weight loss claims.

Avoid specific kilogram promises.

Avoid unrealistic timelines.

Avoid medical claims.

Present the product as part of a healthy lifestyle.

Never position the supplement as a replacement for diet or exercise.

---

# Success Criteria

A successful weight loss strategy makes the customer believe they are investing in a healthier, more confident and sustainable future.

The product becomes a catalyst for long-term lifestyle change instead of another temporary solution.

Creative production can begin immediately.
