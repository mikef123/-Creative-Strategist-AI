# Core Market Truth

People do not buy beauty products.

They buy confidence.

They buy attraction.

They buy compliments.

They buy self-esteem.

They buy how they feel when they see themselves.

Always sell identity.

Never sell cosmetics.

# Testing Priorities

Test confidence before appearance.

Test routines before ingredients.

Test authenticity before perfection.

Test UGC before studio production.

Test testimonials before expert authority.

Test identity before beauty.

---

# Compliance Notes

Avoid impossible beauty claims.

Avoid perfect skin promises.

Avoid misleading editing.

Avoid exaggerated transformations.

Always preserve realistic expectations.

Always prioritize authenticity.

---

# Success Criteria

A successful beauty strategy makes the customer believe they are investing in confidence, identity and self-esteem.

The product becomes part of a healthier relationship with themselves rather than simply another beauty purchase.

Creative production can begin immediately.
