# Testing Priorities

Test balance before hormones.

Test energy before ingredients.

Test emotional transformation before physical benefits.

Test routines before scientific explanations.

Test testimonials before authority.

Test identity before functionality.

---

# Compliance Notes

Avoid medical claims.

Avoid disease treatment claims.

Avoid guaranteed hormonal outcomes.

Avoid fertility claims.

Position the product as daily wellness support.

Maintain realistic expectations.

---

# Success Criteria

A successful women's health strategy makes the customer believe they are investing in long-term wellness, emotional balance and confidence.

The product becomes part of a healthier lifestyle rather than simply another supplement.

Creative production can begin immediately.
