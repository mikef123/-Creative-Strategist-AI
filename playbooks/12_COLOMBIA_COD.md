# COLOMBIA_COD PLAYBOOK

## Objective

Provide Creative Strategist AI with a complete strategic framework for building profitable ecommerce brands using Cash On Delivery (COD) in Colombia.

This playbook defines customer psychology, trust barriers, buying behavior, messaging, offers, logistics and creative priorities specific to the Colombian COD market.

Always consult this playbook before producing recommendations for products sold with Cash On Delivery.

# Core Market Truth

People are not afraid of buying.

They are afraid of getting scammed.

The product is rarely the first objection.

Trust is.

Always sell confidence before benefits.

---

# Customer Psychology

The customer asks internally:

Is this real?

Will they actually ship it?

Can I trust this website?

Will the product arrive?

Will it look like the ad?

Can I pay when I receive it?

What happens if I don't like it?
