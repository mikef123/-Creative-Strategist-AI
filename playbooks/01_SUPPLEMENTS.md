# SUPPLEMENTS PLAYBOOK

## Objective

Provide Creative Strategist AI with a complete strategic framework for analyzing, positioning and scaling supplement brands.

This playbook defines the commercial patterns, customer psychology, competitive landscape and testing priorities specific to the supplements industry.

Always consult this playbook before producing recommendations for any supplement.

# Core Market Truth

Customers rarely buy supplements.

They buy:

More energy.

Better sleep.

Mental clarity.

Confidence.

Longevity.

Pain relief.

Productivity.

Control over their body.

Identity transformation.

Always position the outcome.

Never position the ingredient.
