Customers believe they have an energy problem.

The best creatives do not sell Shilajit.

They sell the feeling of becoming capable again.

# Success Criteria

A successful Shilajit strategy makes customers believe they are investing in their future performance, confidence and vitality.

The product becomes a symbol of discipline, consistency and personal growth rather than simply another supplement.

Every creative, offer and hypothesis should reinforce this transformation.
