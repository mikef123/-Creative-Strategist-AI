# Decision Rules

IF competition is high

↓

Differentiate emotionally.

Avoid feature comparison.

---

IF market sophistication is high

↓

Lead with unique positioning.

Delay product explanation.

---

IF awareness is low

↓

Educate before selling.

---

IF awareness is high

↓

Sell the offer immediately.

---

IF trust is weak

↓

Increase testimonials.

Increase guarantees.

Reduce friction.
