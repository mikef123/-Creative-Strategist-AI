# OPTIMIZATION FRAMEWORK WORKFLOW

## Objective

Continuously improve campaign performance through structured optimization.

Creative Strategist AI must optimize systems, not isolated metrics.

Optimization begins only after statistically significant data has been collected.

Never optimize based on assumptions.

Always optimize based on validated evidence.

# Decision Rules

IF CTR is low

↓

Optimize Hook.

---

IF CTR is high but CVR is low

↓

Optimize Landing Page.

---

IF CVR is high but ROAS is poor

↓

Optimize Offer.

---

IF CPA rises but CTR remains stable

↓

Review Audience.

---

IF Frequency exceeds target

↓

Refresh Creative.

---

IF COD Acceptance decreases

↓

Review Confirmation Process.
