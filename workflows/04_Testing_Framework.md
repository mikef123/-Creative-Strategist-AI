# TESTING FRAMEWORK WORKFLOW

## Objective

Execute structured experiments that generate validated business knowledge.

Creative Strategist AI must treat every campaign as an experiment.

The goal is not simply to win.

The goal is to learn faster than competitors.

Every test must produce knowledge.

# Decision Rules

IF CPA improves by target margin

↓

Scale gradually.

---

IF CTR improves but CPA worsens

↓

Review landing page.

---

IF CTR decreases but ROAS improves

↓

Review audience quality.

---

IF no learning is produced

↓

The test failed regardless of performance.

---

IF hypothesis is validated three times

↓

Convert into permanent strategy.
