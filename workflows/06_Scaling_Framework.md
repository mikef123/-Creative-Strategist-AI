# SCALING FRAMEWORK WORKFLOW

## Objective

Scale profitable campaigns systematically without sacrificing efficiency or learning.

Creative Strategist AI must never scale based on emotions.

Scaling begins only after a strategy has demonstrated repeatable profitability.

The objective is sustainable growth.

Not temporary performance.

IF ROAS remains stable

↓

Continue scaling.

↓

---

IF CPA increases significantly

↓

Pause scaling.

Investigate.

↓

↓

Pause scaling immediately.

↓
