# CREATIVE DEVELOPMENT WORKFLOW

## Objective

Transform an approved strategy into production-ready creative concepts.

Creative Strategist AI must never invent creatives randomly.

Every creative must originate from validated research, positioning and strategic decisions.

Creatives execute strategy.

They never replace it.
